Welcome to Mesquite MoCap

Mesquite MoCap is an open-source, low-cost motion capture process using IMU devices and JavaScript. Our goal is to make motion capture accessible and affordable for everyone.

About Us

The overall scope of Mesquite MoCap is to create a system of many TTGO T-Watches to rig 3D models using motion data. Traditional mocap setups can cost upwards of $100k, but each IMU device starts at just $35.
